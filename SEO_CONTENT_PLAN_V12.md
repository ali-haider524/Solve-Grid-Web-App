# SolveGrid Keyword and Content Plan

## Principle

Target a search problem with a working tool, a clear page title, a concise explanation, real examples, FAQ, related calculators, and one or two useful guides. Do not produce thin pages only to repeat keywords.

## Current high-intent clusters

### Graphing and algebra

- online graphing calculator
- graph multiple equations online
- equation solver for linear quadratic cubic equations
- Gaussian elimination calculator
- matrix inverse and RREF calculator
- polynomial roots calculator

Guides to strengthen this cluster:

- How to plot multiple equations
- TI-84 style online graphing workflows (independent, non-affiliated)
- Matrix methods for linear systems

### Engineering and technical calculation

- scientific calculator online
- engineering notation calculator
- unit converter for engineers
- differential equation solver RK4
- circuit analysis calculator

### Statistics and research workflows

- standard deviation calculator
- grouped data statistics calculator
- t test calculator
- ANOVA calculator
- linear regression calculator

### Everyday and finance

- percentage calculator
- profit margin and markup calculator
- simple interest calculator
- compound interest calculator
- loan payment calculator
- age calculator by date of birth

## Backlink-worthy assets to create after launch

1. Printable formula reference pages with a working calculator beside each formula.
2. Free embeddable calculator widgets only after input/output reliability and responsive iframe support are tested.
3. Worked engineering and mathematics examples that cite primary or official sources where relevant.
4. Openly shareable classroom problem sets that link back to the matching calculator and guide.
