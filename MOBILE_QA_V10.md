# Mobile QA V10

Test at 320px, 360px, 375px, 390px, 412px, 768px, and desktop.

## Navigation
- Header stays one row high on mobile.
- Hamburger drawer opens without pushing a tool down.
- Each category is collapsed initially and can expand independently.
- No category title, icon, or link overlaps.

## Research labs
- Result cards stack after controls but retain concise output metrics.
- Constraint rows remain readable at 390px; at 360px they wrap rather than overflow the page.
- Textareas, matrices, and tables scroll inside their own cards.
- Research explanations sit below the main tool workspace, not before it.
